MOPSI Project


Reinforcement learning for autonomous vehicles

On this page, you will find our results. Our project deals about autonomous vehicles and trafic jams. The main idea was to train an autonomous agent which is able :
1) To drive smoothly according to a specific driving policy
2) To solve the annoying issue of trafic jams in a round with a single lane
Unfortunatly, answering the second point was a tough work and the results are quite disappointing. However, we are glad to present our research and every interesting (and sometimes funny) vehicles that we have trained through our Mopsi.




Entrainement d'un véhicule sur une route simple

Dans cette situation, on entraine notre agent sur une route sans véhicules à une voie. Son reward est construit en suivant les points suivants :
- Vitesse (++)
- Accélération (-)
- véhicule centré (--)
- sortie de route (---)
